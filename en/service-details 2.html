More information

What goods are not accepted for transportation?
1) Weapons of all types: firearms, gas, pneumatic, cold, mechanical and others. 2) Explosives, explosives, as well as all types of pyrotechnic products 3) Compressed and liquefied gases 4) Flammable and flammable liquids 5) Flammable and combustible solids 6) Poisonous and toxic substances 7) Caustic and corrosive substances 8) Animals, plants 9) Human organs 10) Narcotic drugs and psychotropic substances; 11) Ethyl alcohol, alcoholic products 12) Cigarette products
How do you know that the cargo has been delivered?
You will receive an SMS about the delivery of the cargo to the city of receipt, and our managers will contact you when the cargo is ready for delivery.
Can I be sure of the safety of the cargo?
Of course, because our logistics company "EOSTS" has been one of the leading companies for 10 years and we are successfully fulfilling our duties. Trust us and your cargo will be delivered safe and sound.
