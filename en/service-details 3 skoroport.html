CARRIAGE REQUIRING SPECIAL STORAGE CONDITIONS

The transport of perishable goods is a rather labour-intensive transport service. Its implementation requires the perpetrators to act as quickly as possible, with the necessary expertise and specialized means.

Our services

The company «EOSTS» is reliable, quality. We organize prefabricated transportation, transportation of individual consignments of goods. We guarantee compliance with sanitary and technical standards, required temperature conditions!

  • Requirements for motor vehicles. Motor vehicles with high-quality mechanically refrigerated equipment may be used for the delivery of perishable goods. This is relevant if refrigerated or severely frozen products are to be transported
  • Meeting and shipping the merchandise.
  • Customs paperwork;
  • Registration of perishable goods
